System Demand
Check Demand:
Is the total demand ≤ 60A?
YES:
• All loads remain on the Main Panel (60A).
• Smart Home Panel 2 is connected but not actively supplying additional current.
NO (Demand > 60A):
• Simple Switch disconnects Smart Home Panel 2 from the Main Panel.
• Circuits on Smart Home Panel 2 go into island mode.
Island Mode:
• Battery automatically supplies up to 30A.
• Smart Home Panel 2 manages ON/OFF of connected circuits to keep demand ≤ 30A.
