Demand Response Program 2024

Participants are paid based on the net load reduction they provide, earning $2 per kilowatt-hour of energy shared with the grid.

Program Options

Option 1

Non-combustion resources (e.g., traditional demand response).

Option 2

Demand response aggregators participating in CAISO market.

Option 3

Storage virtual power plant (VPP).
